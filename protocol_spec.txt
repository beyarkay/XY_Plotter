PROTOCOL:

'p' 'u'/'d' \n
set pen up/down

'm' <stepper_1_target_position> <stepper_2_target_position> \n
move pen to target positions

's' <stepper_1_position> <stepper_2_position> \n
set current positions of stepper motors (used in calibration)

This does not move the stepper motors.
This just sets the Arduino AccelStepper internal position variables.
